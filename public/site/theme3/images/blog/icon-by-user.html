Welcome to Aakriti Recruiting Pvt. Ltd.

One of the Best Recruiting Agency from Nepal

We are one of the prominent and fast rising recruitment company in Nepal with an ever-growing list of satisfied clients specialized in the recruitment of technical, skilled and professional manpower around the globe. Moreover, we are instrumental in selecting deserving candidates for placement in their area of expertise.

We aim to provide a Total Recruitment Solution to our clients and work to ensure a successful recruitment. As a professional recruitment company, we put a high premium on the value of trust and faith bestowed to us by both our principals and manpower candidates. Extremely up-to-date and in touch with the advancing technologies, we maintain our networking with partner companies worldwide through the means of e-technologies and conventional communication means as well.
